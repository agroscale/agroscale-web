Crop mapping with Earth Engine
==============================

This subsection has four chapters on crop mapping with Google Earth Engine, an on-line platform for analyzing large amounts of satellite data. It facilitating large-scale data analysis by proving access to satellite data for the entire globe for the past 40+ years.

The first chapter has a `general introduction <gee_intro.html>`__ to Earth Engine , this is followed by an introduction to `crop area mapping <gee_landsat.html>`__ (using Landsat data) and by a chapter on `mapping rice area <gee_sentinel.html>`__, using Sentinel data.
